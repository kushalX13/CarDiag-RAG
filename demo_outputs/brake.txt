
Possible Recall-Related Issue

Query:
brake master cylinder leak into brake booster

Best Match:
20V332000 — Brake Master Cylinder May Leak into Booster

Why It Matches:
The brake master cylinder may fail, resulting in a change in brake pedal travel and feel, increased pedal effort and reduced front brake. This closely matches your query because it directly addresses brake fluid leak into the brake booster.

Other Relevant Recall Candidates:
  - 16V345000 — SERVICE BRAKES, HYDRAULIC:FLUID

Safety Risk:
Potential risks include reduced braking ability and increased stopping distance, which may increase crash risk.

Suggested Next Step:
Contact your dealer to verify if your vehicle is affected, or look up your VIN at NHTSA.gov/recalls.
